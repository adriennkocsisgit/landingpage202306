Don’t be a phishing victim!

Sophisticated phishing scams will often look and feel like a professional email. Stealthy cybercriminals will often copy legitimate emails that people see in their inbox everyday.

Better safe than sorry!

If you receive a notification about an existing web account in your inbox, navigate to the official website manually to check your account instead of clicking links or attachments in emails. 

Remember: Don’t trust every email that lands in your inbox.

Hover over links in emails to preview the URL. If the link address is suspicious, don’t click. Simply visiting a malicious website or opening an attachment can infect your device with malware.
